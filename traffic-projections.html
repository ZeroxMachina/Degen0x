🔗 How to Get the Backlinks
0 → 50 links (Month 1–3) Submit to crypto directories (CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap), post on Reddit r/CryptoCurrency with tools, add to Product Hunt, publish 2–3 "data studies" (e.g. "Which exchange is cheapest in 2026?") — these get picked up organically.
50 → 100 links (Month 3–6) Guest posts on mid-DA crypto blogs, outreach to journalists covering crypto tools, get listed on "best crypto resources" roundups, sponsor a small crypto newsletter (often includes a do-follow link).
100 → 500 links (Month 6–12) One piece of linkable data content (e.g. "State of Crypto Fees 2026 Report") can earn 50–200 links alone. PR to TechCrunch/CoinDesk/Decrypt, partnerships with exchange affiliate programs (they often feature partners), YouTube channel linking back to tools.
Quality over quantity 1 link from CoinDesk (DA 80) beats 50 links from random blogs. Focus on crypto-specific sites, finance publishers, and tools directories. Avoid link farms — Google penalises these fast in YMYL (finance) niches.
