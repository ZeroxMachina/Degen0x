## Newsletter v23 Addendum — Tokenization Hearing Spotlight

### Congress Gets Serious About Tokenization
The House Financial Services Committee convened for what multiple outlets called the most significant congressional examination of tokenization to date. Two bills were introduced: the Modernizing Markets Through Tokenization Act (requiring SEC/CFTC joint study) and the Capital Markets Technology Modernization Act (clarifying blockchain record-keeping for broker-dealers and transfer agents). With $26.48B already tokenized on-chain and Nasdaq having approved tokenized equities, the legislative scaffolding is catching up to market reality.
