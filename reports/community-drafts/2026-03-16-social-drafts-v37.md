## Monday Jun 2 — Twitter Thread (5 posts)

**Topic: Fair Token Launches — The Anti-Rug Playbook**

🧵 1/ The insider-heavy token launch is dying.

In 2026, the protocols that win are the ones that launch fair.

Here's the playbook — lockdrops, LBPs, and why distribution is the new moat 👇

🧵 2/ The four fair launch models:

🔒 **Lockdrop** — Lock liquidity for a set period, receive tokens proportionally. No pre-sale. No VCs cutting the line.

🎲 **Lottery** — Random allocation, equal chance. Eliminates whale advantage.

📈 **LBP (Liquidity Bootstrapping Pool)** — Dutch-auction style price discovery. Market sets the price, not insiders.

🔄 **Airdrop-first** — Community owns the supply from Day 1. Protocol earns loyalty before revenue.

🧵 3/ Why fair launches win long-term:

- Wider initial distribution = more aligned holders
- No VC unlock cliffs = less sell pressure at month 6
- Community-first = organic marketing from actual users
- Price discovery happens in public, not in group chats

The math is simple: fair distribution → stronger communities → sustainable price action.

🧵 4/ Real examples in 2026:

- Hyperliquid: No VC round. Largest airdrop ever ($1.6B+). Community-owned from Day 1.
- Jupiter: JUP airdrop to every Solana user — largest on the chain
- EigenLayer: Restaking rewards to early depositors before token launch
- Blast: Points → airdrop model with lockdrop mechanics

The pattern is clear: the best-performing tokens of this cycle launched fair.
