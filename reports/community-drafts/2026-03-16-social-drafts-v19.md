Thread: Restaking has locked up $20B+ in 2026.

1/ You stake ETH to secure Ethereum.

→ You deposit LST (stETH, rETH, cbETH) or native ETH

→ Total Value Restaked (TVR): $20B+

→ **EigenLayer** — original, most AVSs, highest TVL
→ **Symbiotic** (backed by Paradigm) — multi-asset restaking, non-ETH collateral
→ **Karak** — cross-chain restaking, aggressive incentive programs
→ **Jito Restaking** — Solana native, SOL-based restaking model

Different architectures. Same core thesis: reuse security, multiply yield.

→ **Slashing contagion** — a buggy AVS slashes across all operators who opted in
→ **Operator concentration** — top 5 operators control 60%+ of restaked ETH
→ **AVS insolvency** — AVSs pay rewards from their own token; if token crashes, yields evaporate
→ **Smart contract layering** — LRT → EigenLayer → AVS = 3+ contracts between you and your ETH

Higher yield = higher surface area for failure.
