## 1. Twitter Thread — Ethereum Pectra Upgrade Guide (9 posts)

**Post 1 (Hook)**
Ethereum's biggest upgrade in years drops in ~30 days.

Here's what actually changes for you 🧵

**Post 2**
First: what is Pectra?

Pectra = Prague (execution layer) + Electra (consensus layer)

It's a dual-layer upgrade — touching both how Ethereum processes transactions AND how validators stake.

Combined: 11 EIPs shipping simultaneously. It's the most complex coordinated upgrade since the Merge.

**Post 3**
EIP-7702: Smart wallets come to every Ethereum account.

Today, your ETH wallet is "dumb" — it can only sign transactions you initiate.

After Pectra:
→ Batch multiple transactions in one click
→ Sponsor gas fees for users (pay in any token)
→ Set spending limits and session keys
→ Enable social recovery

Every EOA becomes a smart contract wallet, temporarily, per transaction.

**Post 4**
Why EIP-7702 is a big deal.

DeFi UX has been terrible for 6 years.

"Approve + Swap" = two separate transactions, two gas fees, two confirmations.

After Pectra: one click.

For builders: this unlocks seamless onboarding. For users: crypto finally feels like software.

**Post 5**
EIP-7251: Validator consolidation up to 2,048 ETH.

Today's limit: 32 ETH max per validator.

Staking 10,000 ETH? That's 312 separate validators to manage.

After Pectra: one validator can hold up to 2,048 ETH.

→ 90% fewer validator keys for large stakers
→ Reduced network message load
→ Lower operational overhead for staking providers (Lido, Rocket Pool, Coinbase)

**Post 6**
EIP-7691: Doubled blob throughput.

Blobs = how Ethereum rolls up L2 data to L1 cheaply.

Pectra doubles the target blob count: 3 → 6 per block.

Impact: Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, zkSync transaction fees get cheaper again.

This is the "users will notice in their wallet" change.

**Post 7**
What about the rest of the 11 EIPs?

Other notable changes:
→ EIP-6110: Faster validator deposits (from ~16 hours to minutes)
→ EIP-7002: Validators can trigger exits via execution layer (huge for restaking)
→ EIP-2537: BLS precompile — accelerates zero-knowledge proof verification

The cumulative effect: Ethereum becomes faster to enter, easier to use, cheaper for L2s.

**Post 8**
Timeline:
→ Sepolia testnet: February 2026 ✅
→ Holesky testnet: March 2026 ✅
→ Mainnet: April 2026 (~30 days) 🟡

Both testnets shipped without critical issues. Mainnet is on track.
